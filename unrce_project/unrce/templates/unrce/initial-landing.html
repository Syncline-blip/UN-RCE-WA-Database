About Us

RCE Western Australia brings together the passion and expertise of researches, educators, students and partners of the University of Western Australia to make change in their regions, nationally and globally.

Goals & Objectives

Broad Goal

Contributing to a more sustainable planet.

How are we doing it

To build the capacity of UWA and partner organisations to support a process of change and embed education for sustainability.

Actions

To support the translation of established research expertise appropriate to the context of Western Australia for application in diverse community setting to support education for sustainability and broader climate action initiatives (e.g. oceans;biodiversity).
